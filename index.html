Our Story

A Greenwood Tradition Since 1962

What started as a hand-built dream by Jim Davis over 60 years ago has grown into one of Greenwood's most beloved institutions. Nestled near Main and Meridian Streets in downtown Greenwood, Mrs. Curl has been the sweet destination for families across Johnson County for generations.

Open every March through October, we serve the finest in desserts and outdoor fare — from our signature soft-serve to grilled hot dogs and fresh-cut fries enjoyed at our outdoor café beside a peaceful stream.
