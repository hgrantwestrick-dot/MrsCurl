Who We Are

Mrs. Curl Ice Cream Shop & Outdoor Café

Mrs. Curl has been a beloved Greenwood institution for over 60 years. Nestled near the corner of Main and Meridian Streets in the heart of downtown Greenwood, Indiana, we are a true family business — open seasonally from March through October.

Whether you're stopping in for a classic soft-serve cone, a hearty outdoor lunch at our café tables, or just looking for a sweet afternoon treat, Mrs. Curl is the place generations of families have returned to year after year.

Enjoy your treat outdoors near our peaceful stream, under the gazebos, while listening to nostalgic 1960s music — or watch the ducks by the water feature. It's more than ice cream. It's a Greenwood experience.

Our History

How It All Began

The story of Mrs. Curl starts in 1962, when founder Jim Davis saw an opportunity that others might have overlooked. His father had acquired a property that came equipped with existing ice cream machines — and Jim had a vision.

With remarkable ingenuity, Jim designed the building himself. He drove to a nearby Dairy Queen, measured the structure carefully, and sketched out his own plans on paper. Then he got to work — handling the carpentry and finishing himself, with help from friends. Every nail, every board was a personal investment.

Most remarkably, Jim self-funded the entire construction — a true testament to hard work, grit, and belief in the community he was building for. The shop first opened as Parkview Dairy, became Mr. Curl, and eventually settled into the beloved name we know today.

"I think I could be onto something good!"

— Jim Davis, Founder, 1962

Today, the name may have evolved, but the commitment to quality, community, and a great scoop of ice cream never has. Come see us at 259 S. Meridian — and become part of the story.
