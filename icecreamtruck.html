About Mini Curl

Mrs. Curl on Wheels

The Mini Curl is Mrs. Curl's dedicated ice cream truck — bringing all the flavors and fun of our downtown Greenwood shop directly to your event. Whether it's a birthday party, corporate picnic, school event, or neighborhood gathering, the Mini Curl has been a crowd-pleasing hit for years.

Serving the same signature soft-serve, dipped cones, and specialty treats that Greenwood has loved since 1962 — just delivered right to your door.

Note: The Mini Curl is currently out of commission. For inquiries about future availability, please reach out via email and we'll be happy to keep you informed.
